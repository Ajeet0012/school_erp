Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset a student's password? expand_more
Go to the Student Information module, search for the student profile, click on "Edit Profile", and select the "Account Settings" tab. You'll find a "Reset Password" button there.
Can I export attendance reports to Excel? expand_more
Yes! Navigate to Reports > Attendance Reports. Select your date range and class, then click the "Export" button in the top right corner and choose "Excel (.xlsx)".
How do I add a new fee category? expand_more
Admin access is required. Go to Finance > Fee Masters. Click "Add New Category", define the name (e.g., "Library Fine"), and save. You can then assign amounts to this category in the Fee Structure settings.
